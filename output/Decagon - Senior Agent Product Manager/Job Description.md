## About Decagon

Series C enterprise AI agent startup backed by a16z, Bain Capital Ventures, Accel. Core product is enterprise-grade AI customer service agents deployed at F500 scale. Direct competitors: Sierra.ai, Ada. Hiring aggressively across Agent PM tiers (Agent PM → Senior → Strategic → Staff).

## What the role does

The Senior Agent PM embeds deep into a strategic enterprise customer, designs the AI agent with them from prompt logic to deployment, then runs a tight feedback loop back into Engineering. Half Forward Deployed Engineer, half Technical PM, half Solutions Architect.

## Key responsibilities (from JD)

- Build, design, and optimize enterprise AI agents end-to-end.
- Collaborate with customers on workflows, pain points, and goals.
- Write and test prompt logic for specific customer use cases.
- Serve as strategic advisor for customer AI roadmap.
- Run tight feedback loops with Engineering.
- Represent Decagon externally at deployments.
- HITL / approval workflow design.

## Requirements

- 6+ years relevant experience (PM, EM, consultant, or founder background accepted).
- Track record shipping enterprise-grade SaaS.
- Experience with multi-agent orchestration or LLM application design.
- Strong external communication; comfortable as customer-facing representative.
