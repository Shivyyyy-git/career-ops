# Company Research — Decagon

## Basics

- **Product:** Enterprise-grade AI customer service agents, deployed at F500 scale.
- **Stage:** Series C. Investors: a16z, Bain Capital Ventures, Accel.
- **Founders:** Jesse Zhang, Ashwin Sreenivas.
- **HQ:** San Francisco. Secondary office: London (at least one Engineering Manager posting is London-based).
- **Direct competitors:** Sierra.ai (Bret Taylor's agent company), Ada, Forethought.

## Why the timing is right

Decagon is running **Agent PM, Senior Agent PM, Strategic Agent PM, and Staff Agent PM** postings simultaneously — they are scaling the Agent PM function aggressively, not backfilling a single seat. Healthy hiring signal; room to land at the Senior tier without being the only hire.

## Comp

**Interpretation:** Agent PM $240–$285K is likely base + bonus; Levels.fyi $451K–$644K captures full TC including RSUs. Senior Agent PM tier likely lands $280K–$350K base + significant equity, targeting $400K–$500K+ TC.

## Culture / working style

- Heavy in-office in SF per public signal.
- Customer-embedded PM model — not a generalist product org, Agent PMs go deep with one customer at a time.
- Builders-over-specifiers culture: the JD explicitly calls out "write and test prompt logic," meaning IC shipping is expected at PM level.

## Public sponsorship signal

Sparse. No clear public record of H-1B sponsorship. Apply, then ask directly on first recruiter call. STEM OPT buys runway.
