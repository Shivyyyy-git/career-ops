Late-stage fintech ($13B+ valuation reported). Spend management and corporate-card platform processing $100B+ annualized across 50K+ companies. Competes with Brex. Known as a top-quartile comp payer with a builder-first culture and unlimited AI token usage as a listed perk.

## What the role does

Generalist PM slotted into one of 8 product areas (Spend Management, Procurement, Treasury, Bill Pay, Risk, Growth, Accounting, Travel) based on team fit. This is an evergreen pipeline post, not a single headcount — hiring across APM → Senior → Staff levels simultaneously. Small cross-functional pods (PM + engineers + designers).

## Key responsibilities (from JD)

- Own end-to-end product vision and execution for a product area.
- Build high-velocity with a small team; ship, measure, iterate.
- Use customer insights to discover technical possibilities.
- Prototype rapidly with modern AI tools (Cursor, Bolt, Claude Code).
- Evaluate LLM behavior (prompting, retrieval, evaluation, fine-tuning) as a first-class PM skill.
- Translate between technical and non-technical stakeholders; tell the product story.

## Requirements

- BA/BS in a technical field.
- Minimum 4 years of PM experience OR adjacent product-building experience (founder / lead-engineer / product-designer backgrounds count).
- Hands-on passion for AI and building.
- LLM fluency across prompting, embeddings, retrieval, evaluation.
- Cross-functional leadership of small teams.
